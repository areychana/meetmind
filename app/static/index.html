Features

  • Real-time voice AI powered by Gemini Live API
  • Speak mid-meeting — MeetMind answers privately in your ear
  • Screen context — share your screen and ask "what do you see?"
  • Type questions when you can't speak out loud
  • Instant interruption — speak to cut off the AI mid-sentence
  • English-only responses for clarity and consistency
  • Full conversation transcript in real time

How it works

MeetMind sits silently in your browser while you attend meetings on Zoom, Teams, Meet, or any platform.

  • Click Start Session to connect to Gemini Live
  • Whisper or type a question anytime during your meeting
  • MeetMind responds privately through your speakers or earphones
  • Optionally share your screen for visual context
  • Click End Session when you're done

About MeetMind

Built for the Gemini Live Agent Challenge hackathon. MeetMind is a real-time AI meeting co-pilot that uses Google's ADK and Gemini Live API for ultra-low-latency voice interactions.
